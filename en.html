Welcome to King of Glory

A spiritual home to grow in Christ.

We are glad you found us. King of Glory Church is located in Singapore, reaching out to the Indian community with the love of Christ. We hold Tamil and English services, and anyone with a heart for Christ is welcome.

We are focused on super-structuring the body of Christ in the Word of God, prayer, and fellowship. If you are looking for a place to grow in Christ, you are in the right place. Visit us and be blessed.

Our yearly highlights

Seasons of seeking God together

Throughout the year we set apart dedicated times to fast, pray, and draw nearer to the Lord as one family.

Chinese New Year Fasting Prayer

Over the three days of Chinese New Year, church members come together to fast and pray, interceding for God's blessings on families and the year ahead.

40 Days Fasting Prayer

Held every evening leading up to Good Friday, a dedicated time to draw nearer to God as we reflect on His unconditional love and the sacrifice of Jesus. Through fasting and prayer, we humble our hearts, seek His guidance, and align our lives with His will.

Weekly

Faith Walk

A weekly walk where our community prays fervently for God's Gospel to touch the hearts and lives of every individual and family in each home we pass. As we come together in this shared act of prayer and reflection, we are united in hope for a brighter future, not just a walk, but a journey towards spiritual growth and communal solidarity.

Apostle Vincent

Senior Pastor & Founder

Our Senior Pastor
“Engaging God's world through faith.”

Apostle Vincent is the Senior Pastor and President of King of Glory International Ministries. A Design Engineer by qualification who served 20 years in the precision industry, he was called by God in 1998 and stepped into full-time ministry in 2003. Led by God, he began King of Glory Church in September 2000, and has shepherded it ever since.
