We saved you a seat

Come, behold the King of Glory. Here's everything you need to join us this week, across both of our campuses.

“I was glad when they said unto me, Let us go into the house of the LORD.”Psalm 122:1

Come as you are

Anyone with a heart for Christ is welcome. There's no dress code and no pressure, just a warm family glad you came.

Tamil & English

We worship in both Tamil and English across the week, so you can gather in the language that's home to you.

Plenty of times

Three Tamil services every Sunday at Norris Road, plus Bukit Batok, Youth, and weekday prayer meetings. Find a gathering that fits your week.

Two campuses

Our main church in Little India and The Spire in Bukit Batok. Choose whichever is closest to you.
